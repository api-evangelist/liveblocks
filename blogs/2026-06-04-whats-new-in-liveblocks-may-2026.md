Liveblocks released new guides and examples for building AI agents into Comments and creating collaborative grids with Handsontable, alongside completing a migration to an improved v2 storage engine delivering faster connection times and support for larger documents. A new room.history.disable method allows developers to make AI-generated changes that bypass the undo/redo history stack, and copy-to-clipboard documentation prompts make it easier to integrate Liveblocks through AI coding tools.
