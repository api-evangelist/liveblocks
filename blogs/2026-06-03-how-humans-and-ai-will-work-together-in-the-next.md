Liveblocks built an AI slideshow editor demonstrating real-time collaboration between people and AI agents, arguing that the best apps are becoming places where people and AI work together in realtime. Three key principles emerge: agents should operate as visible participants rather than invisible background processes, sync engines are essential for simultaneous editing without data loss, and AI requires sufficient context to function effectively within collaborative spaces.
